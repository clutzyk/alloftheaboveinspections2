How to Choose a Home Inspector in New Smyrna Beach (Checklist)

Updated 2025-08-17 • 5‑minute read

  1. Verify certifications (InterNACHI/ASHI).
  2. Ask for a sample report — look for clear photos and plain‑English recommendations.
  3. Confirm add‑ons you may need: 4‑Point, Wind Mitigation, Pool/Spa, Mold testing.
  4. Check turnaround time — same‑day reports help with tight contingency deadlines.
  5. Make sure the inspector is insured and communicates well with realtors.
