Florida Home Inspection Checklist

Published 2025-08-18

  • Unlock gates, electrical panels, attic and garage.
  • Replace dead batteries in detectors; replace burned bulbs.
  • Clear access to water heater, HVAC air handler and condenser.
  • Run all fixtures; note any chronic drips or slow drains.
  • Label any recent repairs or upgrades with dates and permits if available.
